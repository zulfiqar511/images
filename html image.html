Karachi (Urdu: کراچی‎; Sindhi: ڪراچي; ALA-LC: Karācī, IPA: [kəˈraːtʃi] (About this soundlisten)) is the capital of the Pakistani province of Sindh. It is the most populous city in Pakistan,[12][13] and fifth-most-populous city proper in the world.[9] Ranked as a beta-global city,[14][15] the city is Pakistan's premier industrial and financial centre.[16] It is the cultural, economic, philanthropic, educational, and political hub of the country,[17] and Pakistan's most cosmopolitan city.[18] Situated on the Arabian Sea, Karachi serves as a transport hub, and is home to Pakistan's two largest seaports, the Port of Karachi and Port Bin Qasim, as well as Pakistan's busiest airport, Jinnah International Airport.
